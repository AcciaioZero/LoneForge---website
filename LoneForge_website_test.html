Your Arsenal

Everything in One Place

No more juggling fifteen browser tabs. Every tool you need for solo 5E adventure, forged into a single engine.

⚔️

Character Sheet

Create and manage your hero. Track ability scores, HP, spells, equipment, and everything in between. Built for 5E.

Level-up system coming soon
📖

Bestiary

A growing collection of monsters with reworked stat blocks and abilities. Currently covering CR 0–4, with more on the way.

Expanding
🎭

NPC Generator

Generate and save NPCs on the fly — names, traits, motivations, and secrets. Ready for the unexpected encounter.

🔮

Oracle

Ask a yes/no question, roll the dice, and let fate decide. The beating heart of solo play — simple, fast, dramatic.

🗺️

Dungeon, Wilderness & Settlement

Procedural tools to generate locations, encounters, and events as you explore the world beyond the tavern door.

Deeper generation coming soon
📜

Campaign Notes

Keep track of your adventure session by session. Your journal, your lore, your legend — all in one place.

💰

Loot Generator

Roll for mundane and magical treasure without leaving the forge. From copper coins to legendary artifacts.
