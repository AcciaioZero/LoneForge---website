Why Your Support Matters

LoneForge is a free project built with passion and a deep love for solo role‑playing. If you enjoy the tool and want to help it grow, you can support its development with a voluntary donation. There is no paywall or premium version — just a shared desire to keep improving the experience for everyone.

How to Support the Project

Your support helps cover development costs, server expenses, and the creation of new features, biomes, generators, and narrative tools. Even a small contribution makes a real difference.
